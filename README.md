## 🧠 Scenic Mind – The Third Brain

Scenic Mind is a concept based on the idea of **AI as a third brain for product leaders**.

Product leaders constantly balance:

- strategy  
- customer needs  
- delivery constraints  
- risk and trade-offs  

Much of that thinking is fragmented across meetings, documents, and conversations.

Scenic Mind helps organise that context so product leaders can focus on judgement and decision making.

The principle is simple:

Humans make the decisions.  
AI helps structure the thinking.
